Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 28, 2025

Thank you for using TicTac3x5 ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Guest Users: We do not collect any personal information from guest users. Game progress is stored temporarily and removed upon logout.
  • Registered Users: If you create an account, we may collect your username, email, and optional profile picture (stored locally on your device).
  • Device Storage: Accessed only for uploading your selected profile image. We do not access any other data or files.
  • Ad Data: Google AdMob may collect your advertising ID, IP address, and device details for ad personalization. See below for more.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve app features such as user profiles, game history, and personalized gameplay. None of your personal data is shared with third parties by us.

3. Guest vs Registered Accounts

  • Guest: No login required. Your session and progress are temporary and cleared when the app is closed or you log out.
  • Registered: Your game progress and settings may be linked to your login credentials for future access.

4. AdMob and Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob for displaying ads. AdMob may collect information such as: advertising ID, device data, and IP address. You can learn more in Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and any associated data. You can make this request by visiting our Account & Data Deletion Page.

Once submitted, your data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us immediately and we will delete the data.

7. Data Security

We take data protection seriously. We do not store sensitive personal data on our servers. Any profile images are stored only on your local device.
